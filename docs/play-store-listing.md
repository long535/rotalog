**Rotalog - Shift Tracker & Wage Calculator**

## Short Description (80 chars)

## Full Description

### English
Rotalog is a powerful yet simple shift tracking app designed for hourly workers, freelancers, and anyone who needs to keep track of their work hours and wages.

📊 FEATURES:
• Record shifts with start/end times, break duration, and hourly wages
• Automatic wage calculation with UK tax estimation (Tax, N.I., Pension)
• Annual leave and sick leave tracking with balance overview
• Statistics dashboard with bar charts, averages, and custom date ranges
• Multiple job support with separate settings per job
• CSV export/import for backup and sharing
• Photo attachments for timesheets and documents
• Break timer with background notifications
• Shift reminders (1 hour / 30 min before)
• Calendar view for monthly overview
• Dark mode support
• Bilingual: 繁體中文 / English

🔒 PRIVACY:
All data is stored locally on your device. No account required. No data is ever sent to any server.

📱 PERFECT FOR:
• Retail & hospitality workers
• Part-time employees
• Freelancers & gig workers
• Anyone tracking multiple jobs
