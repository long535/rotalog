Rotalog — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 8, 2026

1. Overview

Rotalog ("the App") is a shift tracking and work hours management application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your data.

2. Data Collection

Rotalog does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All data you enter — including shift records, wage information, settings, and photos — is stored locally on your device only using the device's built-in storage (Capacitor Preferences and Filesystem APIs).

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any form of telemetry

4. Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera — To take photos of timesheets or work-related documents (stored locally only)
  • Storage — To save exported CSV files to your device's Documents folder
  • Notifications — To send local shift reminders and break timer alerts
  • Exact Alarms — To trigger shift reminder alarms at precise times

All permissions are optional and the App functions without them (with limited features).

5. Data Export

You may export your data as CSV files using the built-in export feature. Exported files are saved to your device or shared via your device's share menu. No data is sent to any server during this process.

6. Third-Party Services

Rotalog does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs that collect user data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" above and will be available at this URL.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository: github.com/long535/rotalog
